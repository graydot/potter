Why not just use ChatGPT?

You can. Potter just removes the friction.

⚡

No context switching

Potter works inside whatever app you're already in. You don't open a browser, paste into a chat, wait for a response, and copy it back. You press one hotkey and keep typing.

🔒

Your keys, your data

Potter connects directly to OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google with your own API key. Your text never touches a third-party server. No account, no subscription, no tracking.

🛠

Your prompts, your rules

Create prompts for exactly how you write. "Rewrite for a skeptical engineering audience." "Make this sound like me, not a robot." "Translate to Spanish but keep the idioms." Whatever you need.

What's in the box

A native macOS app. Nothing else to install.

  • ✓ System-wide hotkey (Cmd+Shift+9, customizable) that works in any app
  • ✓ Menu bar icon with prompt switching, status, and settings
  • ✓ OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google Gemini support
  • ✓ Custom prompts with CRUD management
  • ✓ Streaming responses so you see results as they arrive
  • ✓ Processing history to review past rewrites
  • ✓ Auto-updates via Sparkle
  • ✓ Open source (Swift, SwiftUI, no Electron)
