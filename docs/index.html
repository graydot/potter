Features

potter.ai:~$ cat Features

⚡

One Hotkey, Zero Friction

Cmd+Shift+9 from any app. Your selected text gets rewritten in-place — no clipboard gymnastics, no browser tabs.

🎯

Lives in Your Menu Bar

A tiny icon that stays out of your way until you need it. Switch prompts, check status, or configure — all from the menu bar.

🧠

Bring Your Own AI

Works with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google Gemini. Use your own API key — no middleman, no subscription, no data harvesting.

⚙️

Custom Prompts

Create your own prompts for different situations: polish Slack messages, formalize emails, simplify technical writing, or anything you can describe.

How It Works

potter.ai:~$ cat How It Works

No workflow changes required. Potter works wherever you type:

1
Select

Highlight text in any app — Slack, Mail, Notes, your IDE, anywhere

2
Hotkey

Press Cmd+Shift+9. Potter captures your text, sends it to your chosen AI model, and waits

3
Replaced

The rewritten text is pasted back automatically. Your original is preserved in clipboard history
